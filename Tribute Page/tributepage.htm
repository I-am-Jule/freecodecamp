Adventure Time

What time is it?

Adventure Time! An animated fantasy series created by Pendleton Ward aimed primarily at children, which has become popular among teenagers and adults. Besides its comedy and its science fiction aspects the show covers topics regarding coming of age.

One of the reasons for Adventure Time's success are the emotions that the viewers can relate to. This is seen by the quotes the show is famous for.
